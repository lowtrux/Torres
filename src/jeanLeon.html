A man Dreamer. Visionary. Passionate. Charismatic. Non-Conformist. A success story. That was Jean Leon, or rather Caferino Carrión – the name he was given at birth. Originally from Santander, he had survived a tough childhood and decided to turn his life around. A time Together with Jean Dean, Jean Leon opened “La Scala” in 1956, a luxurious restaurant that represented the glamour of the 1950s and 1960s. A Wine Setting out to make the best wine. Jean Leon purchased 150 hectares of the very best land in the Penedès (Catalonia). The winery was founded in 1963 and was the precursor to introduce the concept of Single Vineyard Wine
