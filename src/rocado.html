Tequila Rocado is the result of two families joining forces, two families who share the same values and tradition: the family of Rodolfo Gonzlez, master distillers of Mexican tequila for 125 years, and the Torres family, producers of wines and brandies for over 140 years. The two families bring their knowledge together to produce Rocado, a limited-production, icon tequila.
