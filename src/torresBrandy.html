Torres Brandy is the result of almost centuryold mastery in the art of distilling in the Torres Family, as well as the tenacious pursuit of perfection. In 1928, Juan Torres Casals, second generation, began the development of vintage brandy aged in oak from the best white wines of Penedès (Barcelona), collecting Arnau’s de Vilanova humanist legacy, doctor and theologian of the thirteenth century. The work of Juan Torres was soon recognized and his brandies became synonymous of quality. Today,the constant search for excellence transcends with people like Matías Llobet, Master Distiller for thirty years, who has perfected the art of distillation, elaborating, with passion, dedication and a lot of patience, brandies endowed with their own character and elegance. Historical category of spirituous drinks, triggering in the 20s the trend of combined drinks, Brandy is a protagonist today too in the most selected bars in cities of the whole world due to its versatility and unquestionable quality.
