Miguel Torres Chile was founded in 1979 by Familia Torres. After an exhaustive research, the family acquired 100 hectares near the Cordillera de los Andes, specifically in Curic, 200 km south of Santiago. Miguel Torres Chile was the first foreign winery to settle in Chile. Currently the winery is a reference in the production of high-quality wines and is certified under Fair Trade and Organic standards. Familia Torress vision has triggered major projects, such as the rescue of ancestral varieties and the research of unique terroirs in Chile, which have been a great contribution to industry and the world.
