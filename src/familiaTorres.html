A LEGACY BUILT THROUGH THE EFFORTS OF FIVE GENERATIONS

Since its founding in 1870, Familia Torres has combined tradition and innovation with the aim of making premium wines. Familia Torres’s roots in Penedès, near Barcelona, go back more than three centuries. The commitment to protect the land and environment, the pursuit of excellence, and a passion for wine culture have been passed down from generation to generation. Today’s fifth generation, the focus is on making wines from unique vineyards and recovering ancestral varieties in Catalonia
